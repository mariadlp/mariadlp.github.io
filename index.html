About Me

Professional Background

I received my B.Sc. in Telematics Engineering and M.Sc. in Cybersecurity from the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM), Madrid, Spain. I am currently a PhD candidate working as a researcher in the InnoTep group at the UPM. My research focuses on developing innovative tools that bridge the gap between clinical expertise and educational environments, aiming to promote early intervention strategies for children with neurodevelopmental challenges.

Research Interests

My research activity centers on clinical data protection through the creation of collaborative systems in therapeutic environments, leveraging the principles of Zero Trust and Consensus-based Systems.
